Creator's Story

Background Story

I was born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria, as the third of four children. My upbringing was grounded in strong values of integrity, hardwork and resilience, instilled in from a young age. Growing up in a vibrant yet demanding city like Lagos, I quickly learned that perserverance and dedication are the keys to success. These lessons have shaped my character and continue to guide me as I navigate life, purpose and determination.

Education and Career

I am currently a Computer Science student at the University of Ibadan, where I am cultivating my passion for technology and problem-solving. My primary area of interest lies in data science and machine learning, fields that I have actively begun exploring through personal study and practical projects. I am deeply fascinated by the potential of data and intelligent systems to solve real world problems and improve decision making.

Beyond academics, I am also committed to making a difference in my community. As a volunteer at a non-profit organization, I work with other great minds to teach young girls about technology, inspiring them to see the possiblities within the tech space and empowering themto pursue career in STEM. Looking ahead, I plan to major in data science and machine learning, leveraging my growing expertise to drive innovation and contribute to impactful projects in the tech industry.

Personal Growth

I am firm believer in continous growth and self-improvement. Over the years, I have embraced challenges as opportunites to learn, pushing myself beyond my comfort zone to achieve personal and professional milestones. My journey has been fueled by resilience, curiousity and a commitment to becoming the best version of myself. Whether it's acquiring new skills, taking on leadership roles, or mentoring others, I strive to grow not just for my own success but to inspire and uplift those around me. For me, personal growth is not a destination- it's a lifelong journey of becoming.

Passion and Interests

I'm a huge fan of just staying in sometimes, whether it's curled up with a good book or watching movies that take me to different worlds. I love getting lost in stories, whether they are on a page or on-screen. And I don't just like relaxing indoors, i really love teaching little kids too! And whenever I get the chance, I love sightseeing. I enjoy visiting new places and getting to see the world in different ways.

Values and Aspirations

I value opportunities for self-development, and sharing knowledge with others. I aim to make a meaningful impact for my self and people around me.
